Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) that we've gathered for you:

1. What is Aint City?

Aint City is a revolutionary Web3 app that blends social networking with an immersive city-building game set in the untamed Wild West of the internet. Players stake their claim by mining digital resources, constructing saloons, ranches, and crypto mines using blockchain-powered NFTs and tokens.

Connect with fellow pioneers through decentralized chats, form alliances, or engage in rival showdowns, all while earning real-world value in this lawless frontier of the metaverse.

2. Which chain does Aint City use?

Aint City uses Aint Chain for its infrastructure.

Aint City is a Web3 app blending social networking and city-building in a Wild West metaverse, where players mine resources and build with NFTs. Connect, compete, and earn value in a decentralized frontier.
