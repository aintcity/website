Frontend Developer

We are currently looking for frontend developers.

Job Listing: Frontend Developer at Aint City

Location: Remote (Global)

Type: Full-Time

Annual Salary: $70k - $90k

Posted: September 2025

About Aint City

Aint City is a trailblazing social network and game powered by blockchain technology, creating a decentralized platform where users connect, engage, and play. We're passionate about merging community, gaming, and cutting-edge tech to deliver immersive and secure experiences. Join us to shape the future of decentralized social gaming!

The Role

We're seeking a talented Frontend Developer to join our team and craft engaging, user-friendly interfaces for Aint City's blockchain-based social network and game. You'll work closely with our backend team (primarily using Golang) and designers to bring our vision to life. We welcome quick learners skilled in any frontend tech stack who are eager to adapt to our tools and workflows.

Responsibilities
  • Design and develop responsive, high-performance frontend interfaces using modern frameworks (e.g., React, Vue.js, or similar).
  • Collaborate with backend developers to integrate APIs and blockchain-based features for seamless social and gaming experiences.
  • Ensure pixel-perfect, intuitive, and accessible user interfaces that align with design specifications.
  • Optimize frontend performance for speed, scalability, and cross-device compatibility.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code, following best practices.
  • Work with cross-functional teams (product, design, backend) to deliver cohesive user experiences.
  • Stay adaptable, learning new tools and technologies to support our evolving platform.
What We're Looking For
  • Experience: 3+ years in frontend development, with expertise in building responsive, user-focused applications.
  • Skills: Proficiency in modern frontend frameworks like React, Vue.js, or Angular. Experience with TypeScript, CSS frameworks (e.g., Tailwind, Bootstrap), and state management (e.g., Redux, Vuex) is a plus.
  • Blockchain Knowledge: Familiarity with blockchain or Web3 interfaces (e.g., wallet integrations, dApps) is a bonus but not required.
  • Tools: Comfort with standard development tools (e.g., Git, Webpack, Vite, or similar).
  • Mindset: Creative problem-solver, team player, and quick learner passionate about building engaging, decentralized user experiences.
  • Nice-to-Haves: Experience with gaming interfaces, animations, or Web3 frontends (e.g., connecting to Ethereum wallets).
Why Join Aint City?
  • Work on an innovative platform blending social networking, gaming, and blockchain.
  • Fully remote role with flexible hours and a global team.
  • Opportunity to grow with cutting-edge technologies and contribute to a dynamic frontend ecosystem.
  • Collaborative, inclusive culture that values creativity and innovation.
  • Competitive salary, equity options, and benefits (details provided during the interview process).
How to Apply?

Send your resume, GitHub/Portfolio link, and a brief cover letter to jobs@aintcity.com. Tell us why you're excited about Aint City and how your skills can help us build the future of decentralized social gaming!

Application Deadline: Rolling applications - apply soon!
