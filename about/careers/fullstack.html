Full Stack Developer

We are currently looking for full stack developers.

Job Listing: Full Stack Developer at Aint City

Location: Remote (Global)

Type: Full-Time

Annual Salary: $80k - $100k

Posted: September 2025

About Aint City

Aint City is an innovative social network and game powered by blockchain technology, redefining how users connect, engage, and play in a decentralized digital world. We're building a vibrant, secure, and scalable platform that blends community, gaming, and cutting-edge tech. Join us to shape the future of social and gaming experiences!

The Role

We're seeking a talented Full Stack Developer to join our dynamic team. You'll work on both the backend and frontend of Aint City, contributing to the development of our blockchain-based social network and game. While our backend is primarily built in Golang, we welcome quick learners skilled in any tech stack who are eager to dive into our tools and workflows.

Responsibilities
  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend services and APIs, primarily using Golang.
  • Build and enhance user-facing features with modern frontend frameworks (e.g., React, Vue.js, or similar).
  • Integrate blockchain technologies to support secure, decentralized features for social interactions and gaming.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product, design, blockchain engineers) to deliver seamless user experiences.
  • Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code, following best practices.
  • Optimize application performance and ensure scalability for a growing user base.
  • Stay adaptable, learning new tools and technologies as needed to support our evolving platform.
What We're Looking For
  • Experience: 3+ years in full stack development, with proficiency in backend and frontend technologies.
  • Backend Skills: Experience with Golang is a plus, but we welcome expertise in any language (e.g., Python, Node.js, Java, etc.) as long as you're a quick learner.
  • Frontend Skills: Proficiency in modern frameworks like React, Vue.js, or Angular.
  • Blockchain Knowledge: Familiarity with blockchain concepts (e.g., Ethereum, smart contracts, or other chains) is a bonus but not required.
  • Tools: Comfort with standard development tools (e.g., Git, Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines, REST/GraphQL APIs).
  • Mindset: Fast learner, problem-solver, and team player passionate about building innovative products.
  • Nice-to-Haves: Experience with Web3, decentralized apps (dApps), or gaming platforms.
Why Join Aint City?
  • Work on a cutting-edge platform combining social networking, gaming, and blockchain.
  • Fully remote role with flexible hours and a global team.
  • Opportunity to learn and grow with emerging technologies like Golang and blockchain.
  • Collaborative, inclusive culture with a focus on creativity and innovation.
  • Competitive salary, equity options, and benefits (details provided during the interview process).
How to Apply?

Send your resume, GitHub/Portfolio link, and a brief cover letter to jobs@aintcity.com. Tell us why you're excited about Aint City and how your skills can help us build the future of decentralized social gaming!

Application Deadline: Rolling applications - apply soon!
